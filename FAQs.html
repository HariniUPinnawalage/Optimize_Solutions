How do I create a new project?

To kickstart your project in the "Optimize Task Manager," follow these simple steps: First, log in to your "Optimize Task Manager" account. Next, click on the option to create a new project. Give your project a name, like "Optimize Task Manager Demo Project." You can also customize your project's settings, such as privacy options or team visibility, as needed. Finally, click "Create" to initiate your new project board.

Can I invite team members to my project?

In your "Optimize Task Manager," seamlessly invite team members to your project. Access your project board and locate the option to invite members. Enter the email addresses of your desired team members and specify their roles and permissions if necessary. Then, send the invitations, and your team members will receive them promptly.

How do I set project deadlines?

Inside your project board, you can effortlessly add due dates to specific tasks or cards representing your project's deadlines. Open a card or task, and you'll find a straightforward option to set a due date. Define the desired due date for the task or milestone with ease.

What is the difference between tasks and milestones?

In your "Optimize Task Manager," it's essential to distinguish between tasks and milestones. Tasks represent individual actions or work items required to reach your project goals, while milestones are pivotal points or accomplishments in your project timeline, often signifying phase completions or significant deadlines.

How can I track project progress?

Stay on top of your project's advancement within the "Optimize Task Manager" by updating task or card statuses (e.g., to-do, in-progress, completed). Glance at your project boards to get an overview of task progress. Additionally, utilize reports or dashboards to visualize and monitor project metrics efficiently.

Is there a mobile app available for project management?

Ensure you can manage your projects on the go with the "Optimize Task Manager" mobile app. Visit your preferred app store, whether it's the Apple App Store or Google Play Store, and search for the "Optimize Task Manager" app. Download and install the app to enjoy seamless project management on your mobile device.
